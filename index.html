About Me

I am an Assistant Professor at New York University Shanghai.

As an empiricist, I am passionate in applying and developing data science tools to solve novel challenges in business. Combining causal inference and machine learning methods, my work provides practical insights and recommendations for digital platforms and FinTech. My research has appeared in leading academic journals, and has been recognized with multiple awards.

I obtained my Ph.D. degree from the Operations, Information and Decisions Department of The Wharton School.

About Me

I am an Instructor (Assistant Professor from September 2021) of Operations Management at New York University Shanghai.

My primary research interests lie in the field of marketplace analytics. In my recent work, I analyze the operations and economics of various digital platforms using both data analytic and mathematical modeling tools. I am also interested in developing data-driven methods for analysis of online markets. My research has appeared in leading academic journals, and has been recognized with many academic awards.

Prior to joining NYU Shanghai, I conducted my Ph.D. studies at the Operations, Information and Decisions Department of The Wharton School, under supervision of Professors Senthil Veeraraghavan, Ken Moon and Sergei Savin.
